# Across The Ripple 

I came across GameOff 2025 in the middle of my university exams, so I decided to build a 3D game for the first time. <br>
What a better way to spend my time not studying, right? 

## Play 

[Play the Game Here](https://across-the-ripple.vercel.app) <br>

[itch.io/across-the-ripple](https://a56-a5.itch.io/across-the-ripple)

## Gameplay

Try to survive as long as possible. <br>
Your Boat sinks after 60s - collect stars to reset timer. <br>
Use Ripples to navigate your boat towards the nearest star.
